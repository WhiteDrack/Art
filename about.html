Our Divine Purpose

White Drack is a sacred mission to illuminate the world with the eternal wisdom of our ancient scriptures. We share teachings from the Bhagavad Gita, Mahabharata, and Puranas to guide humanity toward truth and righteousness.

"Just as the sun illuminates the world, the wisdom of our scriptures illuminates the path of life."

The Eternal Mission

As Lord Krishna says in Bhagavad Gita (4.7-8):

"Whenever there is decline in righteousness, I manifest myself to protect the good and establish dharma."

Our Scriptural Foundation

We draw wisdom from:

  • Bhagavad Gita: Essence of Vedic knowledge
  • Mahabharata: Teaches dharma through noble lives
  • Puranas: Preserve spiritual heritage
